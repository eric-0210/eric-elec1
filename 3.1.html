TWICE

TWICE is a South Korean girl group formed by JYP Entertainment through the reality show Sixteen in 2015. Consisting of nine members—Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Jihyo, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu—TWICE quickly rose to fame with their catchy songs, synchronized choreography, and charming personalities.

Known for hits like "Cheer Up," "TT," "Fancy," and "Feel Special," TWICE has become one of the most successful and influential girl groups in K-pop. Their music blends pop, dance, and electro elements, appealing to fans worldwide. With a massive global fanbase called ONCE, TWICE continues to break records and redefine the girl group standard in the music industry.
